Mentors

We are looking for upperclassmen at Indiana University-Bloomington, IU alumni and faculty that are/were a part of the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering to act as mentors for underclassmen. Mentors will have the opportunity to help host and run events for underclassmen. Events may include guest lectures, student panels, and 1 on 1 meetings.

Mentees

We are looking for underclassmen at Indiana University-Bloomington in the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering that are wanting mentorship from uppperclassmen, faculty, and alumni. Luddy Scholars Connect is looking to host 2 events during the 2025 Spring semester which may include guest lectures, student panels, and 1 on 1 guidance. Mentees will have the opportuinity to connect with upperclassmen, build their sense of community, and recieve guidance during these events.
